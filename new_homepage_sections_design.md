## Key Design Principles

1. **Lifestyle First**: Every section has lifestyle imagery and context
2. **Indian Context**: Strong emphasis on Indian homes, families, and pride
3. **Consumer Language**: Simple, benefit-focused, not technical
4. **Emotional Connection**: Stories, testimonials, family imagery
5. **Trust Building**: Certifications, testimonials, service excellence
6. **Visual Appeal**: Warm colors, lifestyle photography, modern design
7. **WordPress Ready**: Modular sections, easy to manage

---

## WordPress Optimization Notes

- Each section should be a reusable block/component
- Easy content management for non-technical users
- Image placeholders with clear instructions
- Flexible layouts that adapt to content
- SEO-friendly structure
- Fast loading, optimized images
- Mobile-responsive design
