### Content Type Analysis: **B2B Focused**

Based on the website structure and content, EAPRO's current website is primarily **B2B (Business-to-Business)** oriented, with the following characteristics:

1. **Technical Product Focus**: Emphasis on technical specifications, OEM/ODM services
